    AS/NZS 3500.3 Cl. 5.4.6 — Ac = Ah + ½(Av2 − Av1)

    Dimensions

    Ah — Horizontal Plan Area

    Av2 — Vertical Projected Roof Area

    Roof rise = horizontal span ÷ slope ratio.   Av2 = ridge length × roof rise.

    Av1 — Shielding Parapet / Wall Area

    Parapets or walls that shield the roof from wind-driven rain. Subtracts from Av2.
